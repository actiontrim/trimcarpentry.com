Return Policy

Our Return Policy

At Trim Carpentry, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Returns

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return any unused and unopened products. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, and it must be in the original packaging. Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required to process a return.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable, including custom-made products, special orders, or items that are no longer in their original condition due to misuse or damage caused by the customer.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If your return is approved, a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item, please send us an email at returns@trimcarpentry.com to arrange for the exchange.

Shipping

To return your product, you should mail it to the address provided on your receipt or order confirmation. You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

For any questions regarding our return policy, please contact us at returns@trimcarpentry.com.
